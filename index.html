About Us

Mettler Company Ltd. is a top Cameroon supplier of school science equipment and chemicals, partnering with Irwin Science Education, a global leader in science education.

We offer affordable, quality lab equipment for Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Geology to schools and colleges in Cameroon and beyond, with no minimum orders and 90% shipped within 48 hours.

From balances and microscopes to safety gear and molecular models, Mettler Company Ltd. supplies everything needed to establish, enhance, or upgrade labs with innovation, quality, and care.
