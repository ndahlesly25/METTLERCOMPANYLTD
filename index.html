About Us

Mettler Company Ltd. is a leading Cameroon-based supplier of school science equipment and chemicals. We are proud to partner with Irwin Science Education, a globally recognized leader in advancing science education.

We provide a wide range of high-quality, affordable laboratory equipment for Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Geology, serving schools, colleges, and higher education institutions across Cameroon and beyond. With no minimum order requirements and 90% of orders shipped within 48 hours, we are committed to fast, reliable, and customer-focused service.

From balances and microscopes to safety gear and molecular models, Mettler Company Ltd. supplies everything needed to establish, enhance, or upgrade science laboratories — blending innovation, quality, and exceptional customer care.
